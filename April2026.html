The Role of AI in Innovation

According to recent field experiments, generative AI dramatically improves the creative output (resourcefulness, novelty, usefulness) of a team. But not for everyone.

The largest benefit goes to those who use Metacognitive Skills:

  • Task Planning: Decomposing large problems into discrete steps.
  • Adaptive Prompting: Iterating and refining prompts based on outputs.
  • Evaluation: Rigorously judging the AI output against reality.

The Takeaway

AI does not replace your planning. It acts as a brilliant brainstorming partner to help you connect the dots faster.

The 45-Minute Breakout Challenge

Putting our tools to the test. Let's win the biggest pitch of the decade.

Your Mission

1
The Client

The 2026 FIFA World Cup is coming to Vancouver. We need to pitch them the ultimate, multi-million dollar telecom package.

2
Map the Ecosystem

Who needs what? Sketch out the needs for Broadcasters, Stadium Fans, IoT Smart Transit, and Emergency Response.

3
Innovate with AI

Use Fuel iX / Gemini / Notebook LLM as your partner. Ask them to analyze FIFA's pain points and generate hyper-targeted product solutions to sell them.

4
Present the Pitch

Put your top product packages and your "winning hook" onto 1-2 slides to present back to the group.

Prompting Pro-Tips

  • 👉 Decompose the task. Tell the AI: "First, list the top 3 telecom risks for a massive stadium event. Wait for my reply before solving them."
  • 👉 Use Persona Prompting. "Act as the CTO of FIFA. What are your biggest fears regarding connectivity in Vancouver?"
  • 👉 Offload Divergent Thinking. "Generate 5 wildly unconventional ways TELUS could use IoT to improve the fan experience outside the stadium."
