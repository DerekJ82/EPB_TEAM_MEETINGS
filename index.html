02.

Our Segment Strategy

Four segments. Defined focus areas. Clear zone ownership.

Growth Zone

Enterprise

Large, complex accounts with high strategic value. Our priority is deepening relationships, expanding wallet share, and protecting our most critical revenue base.

Protection Zone

Public Local

Municipal and education sector accounts. The focus is on retention, competitive displacement defence, and ensuring contract renewals well ahead of expiry.

Growth Zone

Mid-Market Large

Our highest-growth opportunity segment. Emerging enterprise accounts where TELUS can displace incumbents and expand from initial wins into multi-product relationships.

Growth Zone

Public Large

Provincial and federal government accounts. Complex procurement cycles with significant contract value — proactive pipeline management and executive engagement are critical.

03.

Pillar 1: Reinvigorate Customer Loyalty

Drive retention, expand wallet share, and execute proactive renewals.

Wallet Share Expansion

Identify cross-sell and upsell opportunities within our existing customer base. Build structured account growth plans that move customers from single-product relationships to multi-product partnerships, increasing ARR per account.

Proactive Renewal Execution

Engage renewal conversations 12–18 months ahead of contract expiry. Use churn propensity signals and account health scores to prioritize at-risk accounts before competitors can get a foothold.

Loyalty Program Activation

Leverage TELUS loyalty programs and executive sponsorship to deepen customer relationships beyond the transactional. Turn our best customers into advocates who actively expand our presence in their organizations.

Retention Unlock Target $8M Revenue protected through proactive renewals and churn prevention initiatives in 2026–2027.

Why It Matters

It costs 5× more to acquire a new customer than to retain an existing one. Our installed base is our most valuable asset — loyalty is how we protect and grow it.

04.

Pillar 2: Transform Operations with AI

Embedding AI across Sales, Revenue Assurance, Order Management, and Support.

Sales & Presales

AI-assisted opportunity identification, account research, and proposal generation. FueliX and custom tooling are cutting presales cycle times and improving win rates across all segments.

Revenue Assurance

Project Maguire targets revenue leakage at the contract-to-billing boundary. AI-driven audit tools surface billing gaps and ensure we capture the full value of every deal we win.

Order Implementation

Automating order validation, provisioning handoffs, and exception handling. Reducing manual touchpoints accelerates delivery timelines and improves customer experience at the critical post-sale moment.

Support & Compliance

BriBot and AI-assisted triage are reducing first-response times and improving SLA performance. Compliance workflows are being automated to reduce risk and free up team capacity for higher-value work.

AI is not a destination — it is our operating model. Every function, every workflow, every touchpoint.

07.

Key Enablers

The platforms and programs powering our strategy from behind the scenes.

PSO 2.0

A modernized Professional Services model that scales delivery capacity, improves project margin, and creates a repeatable commercial engine for complex implementations. PSO 2.0 turns our delivery capability into a growth driver, not just a cost centre.

Project Maguire

TELUS's Revenue Assurance engine. Maguire systematically identifies gaps between what we sell, what we contract, and what we bill — closing leakage that compounds over multi-year agreements and directly improving EBITDA without adding new revenue.

BriBot — RFP Transformation

A suite of AI tools including ANNA (bid analysis), RFP response automation, and pricing/quote tooling. BriBot dramatically reduces the time and effort to respond to competitive RFPs — improving quality, consistency, and win rates simultaneously.

EPB Hub

A centralized intelligence hub giving the team unified visibility into pipeline, accounts, EBITDA, and performance metrics. Built by the EPB team using FueliX, Gemini, NotebookLM, and Claude Code — a live proof point of our AI-first operating model.
